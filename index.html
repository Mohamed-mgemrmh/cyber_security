Definition

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting digital systems, networks, and data from cyber threats such as hacking, malware, unauthorized access, and other forms of cyberattacks. It encompasses a range of technologies, processes, and practices designed to safeguard information and maintain the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of digital assets.

importance

Cybersecurity help us keeping our digital stuff safe. It stops hackers and bad people from getting into our computers and stealing or messing up our information. It also makes sure our online activities like shopping and banking stay private and secure. Overall, it helps everything on the internet work smoothly and keeps our personal stuff safe.
